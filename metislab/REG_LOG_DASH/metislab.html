Intel Unnati - METIS Lab Access

The Model-driven Explorative Technologies and Intelligent Solutions (METIS) Lab, established in association with Intel under the Intel Unnati Scheme, is available for faculty members, research scholars, research assistants, and selected BCA/MDS project students. Students must submit a duly filled Lab Requisition Form and Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA), endorsed by their project guide/mentor. Once signed by the METIS In-charge and approved by the HoD, CSBYC, IT Services will issue login credentials and schedule access. The lab ensures proper usage logs are maintained. For group projects, one student may submit forms on behalf of the group, and access is granted based on availability.
